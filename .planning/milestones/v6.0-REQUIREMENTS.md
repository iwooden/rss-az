# Requirements: Rolling Stock Stars v6.0

**Defined:** 2026-01-28
**Core Value:** Fast, reproducible game simulation for AI training with full rules compliance

## v6.0 Requirements

Requirements for INCOME phase implementation. Enables income collection for all entities with synergy and corporation special abilities.

### Base Income Collection

- [x] **INC-01**: Each entity (player, corp, FI) sums income from all owned companies
- [x] **INC-02**: Cost of Ownership deducted from each company's income based on star color and CoO level
- [x] **INC-03**: Foreign Investor receives +5 base income bonus
- [x] **INC-04**: Positive total income -> entity receives amount from Bank
- [x] **INC-05**: Negative total income -> entity pays amount to Bank
- [x] **INC-06**: Corporation that cannot pay negative income executes bankruptcy procedure

### Synergy Income

- [x] **SYN-01**: Synergy pairs identified between companies owned by same corporation
- [x] **SYN-02**: Each synergy pair counted once (if A synergizes with B, count once not twice)
- [x] **SYN-03**: Synergy income added to corporation's total before special abilities

### Corporation Special Abilities

- [x] **CSA-01**: Prussian Railway (PR) receives +1 per company owned
- [x] **CSA-02**: Doppler AG (DA) doubles printed income of highest Face Value company
- [x] **CSA-03**: Synergistic (S) receives +1 per 2 synergy markers (rounded down)
- [x] **CSA-04**: Vintage Machinery (VM) reduces total Cost of Ownership by up to 10 (min 0)

### Phase Transitions

- [x] **TRN-01**: CLOSING transitions to INCOME (remove temporary end-of-turn logic from CLOSING)
- [x] **TRN-02**: INCOME has temporary end-of-turn logic (turn increment, roundtrip clear)
- [x] **TRN-03**: INCOME transitions to INVEST (temporary until DIVIDENDS implemented)
- [x] **TRN-04**: INCOME is non-player phase (deterministic, follows WRAP_UP pattern)

## Future Requirements

Deferred to v7.0+ milestones.

### DIVIDENDS Phase

- **DIV-01**: Corporations pay dividends in share price order (descending)
- **DIV-02**: Dividend per share >= 0, <= maximum on share price card
- **DIV-03**: Corporation must have enough cash for (dividend x issued shares)
- **DIV-04**: Share price adjustment based on star requirements

### ISSUE_SHARES Phase

- **ISS-01**: Corporations may issue one share in share price order
- **ISS-02**: Receivership corporations must issue if shares available
- **ISS-03**: Stock Masters special ability (no price change on issue)

### IPO Phase

- **IPO-01**: Private companies may form corporations
- **IPO-02**: IPO price based on company color
- **IPO-03**: Share distribution between player and Bank

### END_GAME Phase

- **END-01**: Game end detection (75 share price, game end card)
- **END-02**: Final scoring calculation
