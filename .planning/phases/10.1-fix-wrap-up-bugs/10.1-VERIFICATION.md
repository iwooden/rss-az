### Technical Details

**Root Cause:** The `compute_layout()` function in `core/state.pyx` was missing `is_auction_high_bidder` (1 field) in the `player_stride` calculation, while `compute_player_field_offsets()` included it. This caused:
- Player 0: Correct (no stride multiplication)
- Player 1: Offset by 1 float (reading/writing wrong memory)
- Player 2: Offset by 2 floats
- FI: Corrupted offset (fi_offset depends on players_size which was wrong)

**Fix Applied:**
1. Added `1 + # is_auction_high_bidder` to line 73 of `compute_layout()` in `core/state.pyx`
2. Added `_is_game_terminal()` helper and terminal state check to `phases/acquisition.pyx` to prevent infinite INVEST->WRAP_UP->ACQUISITION loops when all companies removed
